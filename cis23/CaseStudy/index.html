Emily's Elastic Collection

Emily's Elastic Collection - "Good Morning!" explores Chinese image greetings that one receives from their family members on a regular basis.

The three filters are - 'Flowers','Be Safe' and 'Be Happy'

The images are randomly scattered all over the webpage to replicate the chaotic vibe of them images and the frequency they are received in.

You can move the images over on the page as well.

On double tapping the images, speech bubbles describing the image, the message and 'Date and Time' it was sent are displayed.

The pink glow highlights the image you clicked on and plays an automated voice that reads out the message on the image.
